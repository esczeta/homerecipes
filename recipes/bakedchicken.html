Baked Chicken

"What came first the chicken or the egg?"

Oven baked chicken breast that's simple, juicy, and incredibly tasty that's ready to serve in minutes

Ingredients

  • Chicken breast
  • Lemon pepper seasoning
  • Olive oil or unsalted butter

Steps

  1. Season the chicken breast on both sides
  2. Rub oil onto chicken
  3. Baked for 20 mins at 450 degrees
  4. Garnish as desired with lemon wedges/scallion
