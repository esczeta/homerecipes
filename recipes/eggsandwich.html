How to make egg sandwich

Ingredients

  • 3 large eggs
  • Mayonnaise (kewpie)
  • Salted butter
  • Ground black pepper
  • 4 slices of bread

Steps

  1. Gather ingredients
  2. Mash boiled eggs with sugar and salt
  3. Add mayonnaise and mix well
  4. Assemble sandwich: evenly distribute egg salad onto the slice of bread
