Burnt Basque Cheesecake

"Cheesecake makes everything better"

"This cheesecake is the alter ego to the classic New York–style cheesecake with a press-in cookie crust. Inspired by a Basque version, this is the cheesecake that wants to get burnt, cracked, and cooked at high heat. Which also means this is the cheesecake that’s impossible to mess up."

Ingredients

  • 450g of cream cheese
  • 100g brown sugar
  • 3 large eggs (1 at a time)
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt
  • 270g heavy cream
  • 20g cake flour

Steps

  1. Mix cream cheese with brown sugar
  2. Mix in 1 egg at a time
  3. Sift cake flour
  4. Add in the rest of the Ingredients
  5. Baked for 35 mins at 240c or until amber color
  6. Let cool fully then put in fridge
